Blue Mountain Springs is a set of Application Program Interfaces (APIs) to Blue Mountain, the web resource of the Blue Mountain Project, whose mission is to create a freely available digital repository of important, rare, and fragile texts that both chronicle and embody the emergence of cultural modernity.

In 2015, the Blue Mountain Project received a grant from the Center for Digital Humanities at Princeton University to experiment with Blue Mountain Springs.
